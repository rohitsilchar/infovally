About Us

Transforming the landscape of education in India is Infovalley Educational and Research (P) Ltd

standing proud as the gold standard for a changing future & infinite possibilities. Infovalley Educational and Research (P) Ltd was incorporated on 1st of October 2002 at Registrar of Companies, Shillong, and Meghalaya under companies’ act 1956 with its registered office in the city of Guwahati in the State of Assam. Today, twenty years since Infovalley's inception, Chairman Sanjay Aditya Singh's vision to transform the educational landscape of the North East states—from traditional learning to skill-driven empowerment—has not only been met but also surpassed.

"Infovalley Educational & Research (P) Ltd is deeply committed to driving inclusive development and growth opportunities for all, especially in the North East Region of the Country. Our research & Development in Skill Sector is dedicated to the Youths of North East India and untouched Natural Beauty of all the eight states.” - Sanjay Aditya Singh, Chairman & CEO, Infovalley Group.

Statement of Purpose and Vision

Our goal at Infovalley Educational and Research (P) Ltd. is to improve the North East's educational system by offering high-caliber instruction, advice, and long-term action plans that address unemployment and increase employability. In order to facilitate a seamless transition from training to placement and to develop a trained workforce that promotes industry innovation and regional progress, our goal is to provide young people with both soft and practical skills.

Our goal is to empower people via education and skill development so that India can have a better future. Our goal is to build a community where economic success and sustainable growth are derived from education and empowerment by working together with government agencies, academic institutions, and business sectors.
