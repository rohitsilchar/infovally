About Us

Providing full range of high services solution

Transforming the landscape of education in India is Infovalley Educational and Research (P) Ltd, standing proud as the gold standard for a changing future & infinite possibilities. Infovalley Educational and Research (P) Ltd was incorporated on 1st of October 2002 at Registrar of Companies, Shillong, and Meghalaya under companies’ act 1956 with its registered office in the city of Guwahati in the State of Assam. Today, twenty years since Infovalley's inception, Chairman Sanjay Aditya Singh's vision to transform the educational landscape of the North East states—from traditional learning to skill-driven empowerment—has not only been met but also surpassed.

"Infovalley Educational & Research (P) Ltd is deeply committed to driving inclusive development and growth opportunities for all, especially in the North East Region of the Country. Our research & Development in Skill Sector is dedicated to the Youths of North East India and untouched Natural Beauty of all the eight states.” - Sanjay Aditya Singh, Chairman & CEO, Infovalley Group.

Key Features

A National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) Joint Partner Company under Ministry of Skill Development, Government of India. The Courses are approved by NSDC and part of SKILL INDIA MISSION & All Campus of NER are NSDC INDIA - INTERNATIONAL SKILL CENTRES

PPP partner to the Government of Manipur and Tripura to operate the two State Institute of Hotel Management, Catering Technology & Applied Nutrition (SIHM).

First and the only approved ‘Skill Knowledge Provider’ (SKP ID NO – 1-1216639331 – AICTE) by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) under the National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF) From North East India.

Accredited for International Organization for Standardization Certification (ISO 9000 – 2015) from JAS-ANZ (Joint Accreditation System of Government of Australia and Government of New Zealand)

Approved under Skill Development Initiative Scheme (SDIS) based on MES Skills courses approved by (NCVT) under the Directorate General of Training (DGT)

Only Skill Training Company Registered under the North Eastern Industrial Investment & Promotion Policy – 2007 under Ministry of Industries and Commerce, North East Chapter, Govt. of India bearing a Registration Number U74140AS2002PTCOO6920

Associate Hub Partner of Tata Institute of Social Science- School of Vocational Educational, Deemed University, (TISS-SVE). An Initiative proposed by Ministry of HRD Government of India - Approved by AICTE New Delhi & UGC
